Maid-chan
女仆酱


Project Maid-chan is inspired by the AI named "Maid-chan" from the anime The Pet Girl of Sakurasou. The ultimate goal of this project is to recreate her in real life. Unfortunately, Maid-chan is NOT an open source project for now, due to some of her personalized features and her tokens that can't be simply revealed. However, if you are interested in joining this project, please get in contact with the author, he will be more than happy to collaborate with you!

女仆酱这个项目被动画《樱花庄的宠物女孩》中名为“女仆酱” 的AI所启发。这个项目的最终目的是在现实中重现动画中的女仆酱。不幸的是，女仆酱暂时不是个开源项目，因为一些不能对外公开的个人功能和Token。不过，如果你感兴趣的话，请联系作者， 他将很高兴与你合作！
